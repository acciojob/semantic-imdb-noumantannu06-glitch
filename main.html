Dangal

Summary

Dangal (2016) is a biographical sports drama that remains one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time, amassing over ₹2,000 crore ($311–340 million) at the global box office. Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, the film tells the true story of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat and his daughters, Geeta and Babita Phogat

Actors

  • Mahavir Singh Phogat: Aamir Khan.
  • Daya Shobha Kaur (Wife): Sakshi Tanwar.
  • Geeta Phogat: Fatima Sana Shaikh (Adult) and Zaira Wasim (Young).
  • Babita Kumari: Sanya Malhotra (Adult) and Suhani Bhatnagar (Young).
  • Omkar (Nephew/Narrator): Aparshakti Khurana.
