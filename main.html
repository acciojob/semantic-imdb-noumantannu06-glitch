The Shawshank Redemption

Plot Summary

Wrongly convicted banker Andy Dufresne is sent to Shawshank State Prison, where he forms a deep friendship with contraband smuggler Ellis "Red" Redding. Through perseverance and ingenuity, Andy finds ways to maintain his dignity and ultimately seeks redemption in a corrupt system.

Main Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne - The resilient, intelligent banker who never loses hope.
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding - The narrator and Andy's loyal friend serving life sentence.
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Samuel Norton - The corrupt prison warden exploiting inmates for profit.
  • William Sadler as Heywood - A tough inmate who evolves through Andy's influence.
